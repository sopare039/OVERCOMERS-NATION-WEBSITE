WELCOME TO OVERCOMERS NATION CHURCH

Overcomers Nation Church is a community of believers committed to living victorious lives in Christ. Their mission is to raise overcomers who express the glory-life through supernatural dominion and manifestations. The church offers a warm atmosphere for worship, learning, and growth in relationship with God. They preach the gospel of the Kingdom worldwide and have diverse ministries like Jesus Feeds The Hungry Foundation, Covered Women Network, and Achivers Youth Outreach. Whether new to faith or seeking a deeper connection, they welcome people of all ages and backgrounds.

ABOUT US

Our Name

Ebenezer Okronipa Ministries (EOM) is an Apostolic and Prophetic Movement focused on raising overcomers through the teaching of hidden mysteries in the Word of God. It includes Overcomers Nation Churches (ONC), Micro-Churches (ONA), and campus-based fellowships (ONF) led by pastors and coordinators, with ordained full-time pastors overseeing the ministry as recognized leaders.

Vision

To raise overcomers who will tangibly express the glory-life (Zoe) in Christ through supernatural dominion and manifestations in every sphere of life.

Mission

1. Position believers to experience and demonstrate supernatural manifestations through the study of Kingdom mysteries before the return of Jesus (Ephesians 3:3-5).
2. Preach the gospel of the Kingdom worldwide, accompanied by signs, miracles, and wonders (Matthew 24:14).
3. Raise and empower ministers equipped with diverse spiritual gifts for effective ministry (Ephesians 4:8-12).

Ministry Operations

1. Faith Academy- Multimedia ministry (podcasts and picture messages)
2. Prophetic Mandate Meeting (PMM)- Monthly non-denominational Spiritual Upgrade meetings.
3. Ebenezer Okronipa Outreaches- Evangelistic and Healing Outreaches.
4. Mountain of Fire Prayer Movement- A weekly non-denominational intercessory prayer and prophetic service.
5. Prophetic Eagles Convocations.
6. Triumphant Generation Youth Outreach- Non-denominational outreach to the youth and schools.
7. Jesus Feed The Hungry- Humanitarian Activities.

Statements of Faith

1. The Bible is the inspired and infallible Word of God (2 Timothy 3:16; 2 Peter 1:20-21).
There is one God existing eternally in three persons: God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit (Ephesians 4:5-6; 1 Corinthians 8:6; Genesis 1:26; Matthew 3:16-17).
2. We believe in the deity of Christ, including His virgin birth, conception by the Holy Spirit, death, bodily resurrection, and ascension into heaven (1 John 1:1-4; John 10:30; Hebrews 1:1-5; Matthew 1:18-25; Luke 1:30-35; John 19:30-35; John 20:25-29; Luke 24:36-40; Acts 1:9-11; 1 Thessalonians 4:16).

PCGs (Pastoral Care Groups)

The cell system in Ebenezer Okronipa Ministries (EOM) encourages fellowship, growth, and accountability among members, as they meet weekly in small groups called Cell Units to share the word of God, nurture relationships, reach out to others, and eventually form Pastoral Care Groups (PCGs) within the larger church structure, providing opportunities for individual growth and leadership development.
